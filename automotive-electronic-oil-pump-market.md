<p><strong>The Automotive Electronic Oil Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Electronic Oil Pump</li><li>Separate Electronic Oil Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive electronic oil pump market is categorized into two main types: integrated electronic oil pumps and separate electronic oil pumps. Integrated electronic oil pumps are designed as a compact unit within the engine assembly, providing efficient oil circulation while reducing weight and space. In contrast, separate electronic oil pumps operate independently, allowing for more flexible placement within the vehicle and enhanced control over oil flow. Both types contribute to improved fuel efficiency and optimized engine performance in modern vehicles.</p></p>

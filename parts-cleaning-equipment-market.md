<p><p>Parts cleaning equipment encompasses various methods for removing contaminants from industrial components. Ultrasonic cleaning utilizes high-frequency sound waves in a liquid solution, creating microscopic bubbles that dislodge dirt and grime from surfaces. Water cleaning, often employing high-pressure jets or spray systems, effectively removes contaminants using water, sometimes enhanced with detergents. Other methods may include solvent-based cleaning and dry ice blasting, each tailored for specific applications, materials, and cleanliness levels required in industries such as manufacturing, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>

<p><p>The Aircraft Annunciator Panel market is categorized into two primary types: Alarm and Trouble. Alarm indicators are designed to alert pilots to critical conditions requiring immediate attention, such as engine failure or low fuel levels. In contrast, Trouble indicators signal non-critical issues that may require monitoring or maintenance, like system malfunctions or minor equipment failures. Both types enhance situational awareness and safety by providing real-time information to the flight crew about the aircraft's operational status.</p></p>

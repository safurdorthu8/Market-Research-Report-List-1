<p><p>The ampoule filling machine market is primarily categorized into open type and closed type systems. Open type machines facilitate easy access for operators during the filling process but may expose products to environmental contaminants. In contrast, closed type machines maintain a sterile environment, minimizing contamination risks and ensuring product integrity. This makes closed type machines ideal for sensitive pharmaceuticals and biopharmaceuticals. Both types cater to different operational requirements, balancing efficiency, safety, and product quality.</p></p>

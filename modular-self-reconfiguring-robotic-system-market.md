<p><p>The modular self-reconfiguring robotic system market features three key architectures: Chain, Lattice, and Hybrid. Chain architecture consists of linear connections between modules, enabling easy reconfiguration but limiting flexibility. Lattice architecture offers a grid-like structure, allowing for greater adaptability and complex movements. Hybrid architecture combines elements of both chain and lattice, providing a balance of modularity and versatility suited for diverse applications. Each architecture caters to different operational needs, influencing market demand and technological advancements.</p></p>

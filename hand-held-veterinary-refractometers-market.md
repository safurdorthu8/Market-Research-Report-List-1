<p><ul><li>Optical Refractometers</li><li>Digital Refractometers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hand-held veterinary refractometer market is primarily divided into two types: optical refractometers and digital refractometers. Optical refractometers use traditional lenses and prisms to measure the refractive index of liquids, offering a straightforward, analog reading. In contrast, digital refractometers employ advanced electronic sensors to provide precise, easy-to-read digital outputs, often featuring additional functionalities like temperature compensation. Both types are essential tools for veterinarians, facilitating the quick assessment of various fluids, including urine and serum, to monitor animal health effectively.</p></p>

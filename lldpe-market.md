<p><p>The LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) market is categorized based on the carbon chain length of the comonomer used during its production. C4-LLDPE, produced using butene-1, offers high flexibility and strength, making it suitable for packaging films. C6-LLDPE, utilizing hexene-1, provides enhanced clarity and toughness, often used in agricultural films and rigid containers. C8-LLDPE, made with octene-1, offers superior elasticity and impact resistance, ideal for products requiring greater stretchability, like stretch films. Each type serves distinct applications in various industries.</p></p>

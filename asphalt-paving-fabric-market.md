<p><p>The asphalt paving fabric market is categorized into woven and non-woven fibers. Woven fibers are tightly interlaced materials offering high tensile strength and durability, making them suitable for heavy-load applications. Non-woven fibers, on the other hand, are produced by bonding fibers that provide excellent drainage and flexibility. Both types serve to enhance pavement longevity, reduce reflective cracking, and improve overall structural integrity, catering to various construction needs and conditions in asphalt paving projects.</p></p>

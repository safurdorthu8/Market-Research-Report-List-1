<p><p>The RO membrane module market is primarily divided into two types: thin-film and cellulose acetate. Thin-film membranes, made from polyamide, offer higher permeability and rejection rates, making them favored for various applications, including seawater desalination and industrial processes. Other advanced materials, such as ceramic membranes, enhance durability and resistance to fouling. Cellulose acetate membranes, while less efficient than thin-film, are more environmentally friendly and suitable for specific applications, providing cost-effective solutions in water treatment and filtration processes.</p></p>

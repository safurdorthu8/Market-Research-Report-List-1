<p><p>The Defense Fuel Cells market encompasses three main types: Proton Exchange Membrane Fuel Cells (PEMFC), Solid Polymer Fuel Cells (SPFC), and Direct Methanol Fuel Cells (DMFC). DMFCs use methanol directly, providing a simpler fuel handling system, making them advantageous for specific military applications requiring energy density and ease of use.</p></p>

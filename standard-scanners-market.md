<p><strong>The Standard Scanners Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flatbed scanners</li><li>Feed-through scanner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The standard scanners market primarily consists of flatbed scanners and feed-through scanners. Flatbed scanners utilize a stationary glass surface where documents are placed for scanning, offering high-quality images and versatility for a range of document sizes. In contrast, feed-through scanners are designed for high-volume scanning, automatically feeding documents through the device, allowing for faster processing and efficiency. Both types cater to different needs in offices, archives, and personal use, enhancing document digitization and management.</p></p>

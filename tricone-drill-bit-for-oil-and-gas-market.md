<p><p>Tricone drill bits are widely used in the oil and gas market for drilling applications. The two primary types are TCI (Tungsten Carbide Insert) drill bits and Milled Tooth drill bits. TCI bits feature tungsten carbide inserts for enhanced durability and are suited for harder formations, offering longer life and efficiency. In contrast, Milled Tooth bits, made from carbon steel with teeth milled onto the body, are effective for softer formations and provide cost advantages, making them popular in specific drilling scenarios.</p></p>

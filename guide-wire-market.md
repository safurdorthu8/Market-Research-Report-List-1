<p><ul><li>Straight Guide Wire</li><li>Angled Guide Wire</li><li>J-Shape Guide Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The guide wire market includes three primary types: straight, angled, and J-shape guide wires. Straight guide wires are typically used for direct access and navigation in vascular procedures. Angled guide wires feature a curved tip that aids in maneuvering through challenging anatomical pathways. J-shape guide wires, with their distinct hook-like design, facilitate entry into complex lesions and can be particularly effective in guiding catheters. Each type serves specific procedural needs within interventional and diagnostic applications in medical settings.</p></p>

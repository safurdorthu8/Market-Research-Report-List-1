<p><p>The flange sealing market comprises three primary types: metallic, semi-metallic, and non-metallic. Metallic types, such as gaskets made of steel or aluminum, offer high strength and durability, suitable for extreme conditions. Semi-metallic types combine metals with non-metallic materials, providing flexibility and resilience while retaining strength, ideal for variable pressure and temperature. Non-metallic types, including rubber or polymer gaskets, are used for lower pressure applications, offering chemical resistance and ease of installation, making them suitable for diverse industrial environments.</p></p>

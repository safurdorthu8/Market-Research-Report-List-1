<p><p>Turnkey construction of biogas plants involves the complete delivery of facilities that convert organic waste into biogas. The market is primarily divided into two types: wet digestion and dry digestion. Wet digestion processes utilize high moisture feedstocks, facilitating anaerobic digestion in liquid form, making it suitable for wastewater treatment. Conversely, dry digestion involves lower moisture content, enabling the processing of solid organic materials. Both methods efficiently produce renewable energy and help manage waste, addressing sustainability and environmental challenges.</p></p>

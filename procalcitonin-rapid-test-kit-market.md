<p><p>The Procalcitonin Rapid Test Kit market includes two main types: Immunochromatographic and Fluorescence Immunoassay. Immunochromatographic tests employ a simple lateral flow mechanism, providing quick results through visually identifiable bands on test strips. In contrast, Fluorescence Immunoassays utilize fluorescent markers to detect Procalcitonin levels with greater sensitivity and accuracy, often requiring specialized equipment. Both types are essential for diagnosing bacterial infections and sepsis, facilitating timely clinical decision-making and improving patient outcomes in various healthcare settings.</p></p>

<p><p>The microdisplay market encompasses various types, primarily including LCD monitors, Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS), and Organic Light Emitting Diodes (OLED). LCD monitors utilize liquid crystals to create images and are common in displays and projectors. LCoS, a variation, integrates liquid crystals on silicon for high-resolution imaging, ideal for augmented and virtual reality applications. OLED technology offers vibrant colors and deeper blacks by emitting light directly, making it popular for high-quality displays in smartphones and TVs.</p></p>

<p><p>Filter market types encompass various frequency-selective devices. Lowpass filters allow signals below a specific cutoff frequency to pass while attenuating higher frequencies, ideal for audio processing. Highpass filters do the reverse, enabling signals above the cutoff frequency to pass, commonly used in communication systems. Bandpass filters permit a certain frequency range, making them essential in radio frequency applications. Other filters include notch filters that eliminate narrow frequency bands and all-pass filters which maintain amplitude but alter phase, catering to diverse electronic and audio applications.</p></p>

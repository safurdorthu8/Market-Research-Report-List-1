<p><p>The rod ends market is divided into two main types based on threading: internal threads and external threads. Internal thread rod ends feature a threaded hole that can accommodate a screw or bolt, allowing for attachment to an assembly. Conversely, external thread rod ends have threads on the outside, enabling them to be fastened into a threaded hole or component. Each type serves specific applications in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, and machinery, providing essential joint and connection solutions.</p></p>

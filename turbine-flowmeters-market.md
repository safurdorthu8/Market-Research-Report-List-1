<p><p>Turbine flowmeters are devices used to measure the flow of liquids and gases by utilizing the mechanical motion of a turbine that spins in response to fluid movement. As fluid passes through the flowmeter, it causes the turbine to rotate, generating a pulse output that can be correlated to flow rate. These instruments are favored for their accuracy, reliability, and ability to measure a wide range of flow rates, making them suitable for various industries, including oil and gas, water management, and chemical processing.</p><p>The Turbine Flowmeters Market is poised for significant growth, with an anticipated compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 13.5% during the forecast period.

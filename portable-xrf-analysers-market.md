<p><strong>The Portable XRF Analysers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PIN</li><li>SDD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Portable XRF analyzers are categorized into two primary market types: PIN (PIN Diode) and SDD (Silicon Drift Detector). PIN detectors offer lower resolution and sensitivity, making them suitable for general elemental analysis in various applications, often at a more affordable price point. In contrast, SDD detectors provide higher resolution and faster response times, delivering more accurate results for complex samples. This distinction enables users to choose devices based on their specific analytical needs, balancing cost, performance, and application requirements.</p></p>

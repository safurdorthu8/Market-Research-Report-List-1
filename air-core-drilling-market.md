<p><strong>The Air Core Drilling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dust Drilling</li><li>Mist Drilling</li><li>Foam Drilling</li><li>Aerated Fluid Drilling</li><li>Nitrogen Membrane Drilling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Air core drilling encompasses various methods used for drilling in dry conditions, focusing on different techniques to manage the drilling environment. Dust drilling uses a high-velocity air stream to remove chips and maintain a clean hole. Mist drilling combines air and water for better lubrication. Foam drilling utilizes foam for added stability and fluid control. Aerated fluid drilling introduces air into drilling fluids for enhanced performance. Nitrogen membrane drilling uses nitrogen separation to maintain pressure and optimize drilling efficiency in challenging conditions.</p></p>

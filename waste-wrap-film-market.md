<p><p>The Waste Wrap Film market comprises two primary types: shrink film and stretch film. Shrink film is designed to tightly conform around products when heat is applied, providing protection and stability during shipping. Stretch film, on the other hand, is a flexible plastic film that is stretched around items, securing them tightly together without heat. Both types are integral in packaging applications, preventing damage and contamination, while improving load stability and facilitating efficient transportation in various industries.</p></p>

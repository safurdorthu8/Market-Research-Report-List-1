<p><p>Inline flow meters are essential devices used to measure the flow of liquids and gases in various industries. The market is divided into two main types: digital flow meters and mechanical flow meters. Digital flow meters utilize electronic sensors to provide accurate and real-time measurements, often featuring advanced data analytics and connectivity options. In contrast, mechanical flow meters rely on moving parts and mechanical principles to determine flow rates, being more straightforward but potentially less precise than their digital counterparts. Both types cater to different applications and industry requirements.</p></p>

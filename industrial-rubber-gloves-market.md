<p><p>The industrial rubber gloves market features several key types. Natural rubber (latex) gloves offer excellent elasticity and comfort but may cause allergic reactions in some users. Polyvinyl chloride (PVC) gloves are cost-effective and resistant to chemicals but lack durability compared to other options. Nitrile gloves are puncture-resistant, provide a good fit, and are latex-free, making them popular in various industries. Neoprene gloves offer chemical resistance and flexibility, making them ideal for demanding applications. "Others" may include specialized materials for niche uses.</p></p>

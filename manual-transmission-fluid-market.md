<p><p>The manual transmission fluid (MTF) market comprises three primary types: mineral MTF, synthetic MTF, and semi-synthetic MTF. Mineral MTF is derived from refined crude oil, providing basic lubrication and performance but less stability at high temperatures. Synthetic MTF offers superior lubricating properties, better thermal stability, and enhanced performance under extreme conditions, making it ideal for high-performance vehicles. Semi-synthetic MTF blends mineral and synthetic bases, aiming to balance cost and performance, providing improved characteristics over traditional mineral fluids.</p></p>

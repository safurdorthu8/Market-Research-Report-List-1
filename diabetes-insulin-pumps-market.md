<p><strong>The Diabetes Insulin Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tethered Pumps</li><li>Patch Pumps</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Diabetes Insulin Pumps market is primarily categorized into two types: tethered pumps and patch pumps. Tethered pumps are connected to the body via a catheter and are typically worn on a belt or waistband, offering continuous insulin delivery while allowing users to bolus for meals. In contrast, patch pumps are adhesive devices applied directly to the skin, providing a discreet, tubeless option for insulin delivery. Both types aim to enhance diabetes management, improving patient convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
